Frequently Asked Questions

When should I arrive for the event?

Please arrive at 7:30pm on 17 November 2018 at Tower Millennium Pier in London. Our party yacht (the Dixie Queen) will depart from the pier promptly at 8pm.

What is the address of the boarding location?

Tower Millennium Pier, London EC3N 4DT.

How can I get to the event from Oxford/Cambridge?

A chartered bus from Oxford/Cambridge to the event is provided for a small fee. Please click here for more details.

Alternatively, you can make your way to London and take the Cricle Line to Tower Hill Station; the pier is a short walk away from Tower Hill Station.

What attractions are on board?

Dance floor with a professional DJ, photobooth, glitter artist, casino, fortune teller, henna painting, face painting,waltz lessons, jazz band, and more.

What is the dress code for the party?

Black tie; do feel free to come in thematic or traditional dress.

What is the theme of this party?

This year’s theme is JADE - it’s all about extravagance and celebration. We’re bringing Shanghai to London, so get ready to dance the night away on this jazzy night.

What are the ticket prices?

Ticket price information is available at the respective ticketing sites for Oxford and Cambridge.

What does the VIP ticket include?

Please follow our Facebook event page for event updates.

How do I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased here. Tickets will be released on 17 October.

Do I have to bring cash?

Cash payments are recommended on board as there are limited card readers available on the yacht.

Am I allowed to bring my own alcohol? How about food and drinks?

No, food and drinks are strictly not allowed to be brought on board.

Can I invite friends from outside Oxbridge?

Yes, they are very welcome to join us.

When and where can I collect my wristbands?

Wristbands can be collected at the Pier from 7:30pm on the day of the event, as long as you are able to produce your e-ticket.

Where can I get event updates?

Please follow our Facebook event page for event updates.

Am I allowed to smoke on board?

Yes, but only in designated smoking areas.

Can we make song requests?

Yes, please follow our Facebook event page for more details.
