Our events

Parties & socials

Each term, OUAPS holds a series of socials and themed parties in collaboration with other Asian societies in Oxford. They are a great way for members to meet like-minded students - and have a great time!

Food festival

We love our food in OUAPS. Each year, with the help of our partners in Oxford, we organise a food festival filled with mouthwatering delicacies from the Asia-Pacific region.

Formals

Fancy dress and great food - OUAPS brings its members together with culturally themed formal dinners with the help of partner Asian societies in Oxford.

Oxbridge boat party

For one night only, cruise along the Thames on a luxury party boat in the annual OUAPS & ABACUS Cambridge flagship party. Meet new friends as hundreds of students travel across the country for the party of a lifetime.

Speakers

We provide members with opportunities to meet leaders in politics, business and academia from the Asia-Pacific region and beyond. By hosting interesting speakers at Oxford University, we aim to provide fresh perspectives on issues in Asia-Pacific in a global context for our members.
