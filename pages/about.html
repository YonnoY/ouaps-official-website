About us

A home for students from Asia-Pacific and beyond.

We're a community of Oxford University students passionate about Asia-Pacific and beyond. Our mission is to build an inclusive society for both students with Asia-Pacific roots and students with an interest in the region.

Each term, members enjoy a series of social, academic and career events for our members. We also work with various student societies to organise exciting cultural activities.
